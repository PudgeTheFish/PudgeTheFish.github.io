About Me
  • Bio

    I'm a Vanderbilt University senior majoring in computer science. I fell in love with CS while tinkering with my friend's Python hangman app, where I added in some sassy retorts for incorrect guesses. I learned more programming on my own and made the switch from a pre-med neuroscience major to the engineering school, and I haven't looked back since.

  • Experience

    I've interned at Amazon, where I created a new data pipeline for order data. The summer before that, I worked for a research lab on an outreach project to create an augmented reality game as a teaching tool. I also have graphic design experience, and have worked as the Vanderbilt Law School's graphic designer.

  • Hobbies

    In my free time I like to read sci-fi dystopian novels with virtually indestructible protagonists. I've also tried my hand at sewing, henna tattooing, painting, dance, and metalsmithing (some have gone more smoothly than others).
